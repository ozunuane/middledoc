### FAQ Section

**Headline:**
Questions? We Have Answers.

**Q1: How do clients upload documents if they don't have a login?**
- **Answer:** Clients get a unique share link via email. They click the link and upload documents directly—no signup, no password, no account creation. Completion rates jump an average of 27% compared to requiring a login. If you need your clients to have an account for follow-ups, we can enable that, but it's optional.

**Q2: How much does it cost to send e-signatures?**
- **Answer:** Zero. Completely free. Unlike DocuSign (which charges $1-3 per signature), every signature on MiddleDoc costs nothing. Send as many signatures as you want, to as many clients as you want. Your cost stays the same.

**Q3: How does the AI document classification work?**
- **Answer:** Upload a document. Our AI (powered by GPT-4 or Claude) reads it and automatically assigns it to one of 25 categories—tax return, invoice, contract, insurance form, ID, etc. Accuracy is 99% across document types. You can manually override any classification, and we learn from corrections.

**Q4: Is MiddleDoc HIPAA compliant?**
- **Answer:** Yes. We're HIPAA-compliant and encrypt all data in transit and at rest. Healthcare firms, mental health practices, and clinics use MiddleDoc for patient intake and consent forms. We maintain detailed audit logs for compliance.

**Q5: Can I integrate MiddleDoc with QuickBooks?**
- **Answer:** Yes. Firm plan and above includes one-way QuickBooks integration via OAuth. Sync client data automatically. Available for QuickBooks Online; desktop integration coming in Q3 2026.

**Q6: What if my team needs different access levels?**
- **Answer:** Team and Firm plans include role-based access control. Create roles (owner, admin, member), assign team members, and use groups to control which clients each member sees. Audit log captures all actions.

**Q7: Can I customize the email reminders?**
- **Answer:** Yes. Every plan includes 5 customizable email templates. Set reminders at 7 days, 3 days, and deadline. Choose between automated (fires automatically) or manual (you send when ready). All emails are branded with your logo.

**Q8: How do you compare to TaxDome, Canopy, or Liscio?**
- **Answer:** MiddleDoc is 60-80% cheaper than competitors while offering similar core features (client portal, e-signatures, document management). We don't charge per signature. Clients don't need a login. And AI classification is included on every plan. Most firms switch and immediately cut costs by $300-500/month.
